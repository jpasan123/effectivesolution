Route Radar

Mobile Tracking System

Route Radar is a universal tracking platform which can be used to track mobile devices real-time. The platform supports a wide array of mobile devices including GSM, GPRS and GPS technologies to locate the devices. Charges are applied depending on the technology used and the accuracy demanded. The service is publicly accessible via www.routeradar.lk and customized business solutions are provided via b2b.routeradar.lk.

ePro – Energy/Power Monitoring System

IOT platform

“ePro1000” is a product of JLanka Technologies (Pvt) Ltd which enables remotely monitoring the power consumption of electrical devices connected to the grid. The goal of this project is to develop a web application for the buyers of the devices to graphically visualize the performance of each electric device integrated with an “ePro1000” product.

Jendo

IOT platform

Developed a novel product and algorithm to predict cardiovascular diseases before 5 years in advance. This product is a non-invasive analysis of endothelial functions based on the temperature variations and digital volume pulse.

Autobargains

E-Commerce Applications

Auto Bargains offers you fast, free and easy entry to the most trustworthy car auctions in Japan. With more than 150,000 vehicles processed in the auctions each week, you will definitely have a huge range of models, colours, and years to choose from. If you cannot find what you are looking for in New Zealand, you can certainly find it here. All Japanese cars are independently checked by Japanese car technicians before the auction. They record details about the car on an auction sheet. Our auction agents will happily translate these sheets for you. If you are not satisfied with that, they will inspect the car for you. Nothing will be hidden. View the price of any car sold in Japanese car auctions during the last three months to get an idea of the auction prices your type of car fetches, so you can make the best offer.

Health 24x7

Healthcare Applications

Health 24x7 project was meant for creating the largest medical professionals’ database in Sri Lanka. This project was considered a supportive web application for eDoctor Consultancy (Pvt) Ltd. The website is publicly accessible and provides services free of charge. The medical professionals are charged to create profiles in the system.
